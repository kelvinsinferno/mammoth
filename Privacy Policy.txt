================================================================================

This Privacy Policy describes how Kelvinsinferno Studio LLC ("Company," "we,"
"us," "our") handles information in connection with your use of Mammoth Protocol
("Platform"), accessible at mammothprotocol.com.

================================================================================

1. INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT

Mammoth Protocol is designed to minimize data collection. We do not collect:
(a) Your name, email address, or other personally identifiable information unless
    voluntarily provided.
(b) Your private keys, seed phrases, or wallet credentials. We have no access to
    these and never will.
(c) Payment card or bank account information.
(d) Government-issued ID or KYC documentation (the Platform does not perform KYC
    at this time).

================================================================================

2. INFORMATION COLLECTED AUTOMATICALLY

When you access the Platform, the following may be collected automatically:

(a) Wallet Address: Your public blockchain wallet address when you connect to
    the Platform. This is a public identifier visible on the blockchain.

(b) On-Chain Activity: Any transactions you conduct through the Platform are
    permanently recorded on the Solana blockchain and are publicly visible.
    The Company does not control this and has no ability to delete it.

(c) Usage Data: Standard web server logs including IP address, browser type,
    pages visited, and timestamps. This data is used to maintain and improve
    the Platform and is not sold to third parties.

(d) Browser Storage: We use localStorage to store user preferences such as
    theme settings and draft token configurations. This data remains on your
    device and is not transmitted to our servers.

================================================================================

3. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

We use the information described above to:
(a) Operate and maintain the Platform.
(b) Monitor for security threats, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Service.
(c) Improve Platform functionality and user experience.
(d) Comply with applicable legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties for marketing
or advertising purposes.

================================================================================

4. BLOCKCHAIN DATA

You acknowledge that all transactions conducted on the Solana blockchain are
permanently public. This includes token purchases, sales, wallet balances, and
any other on-chain activity. The Company has no ability to modify or delete
blockchain records. Before conducting any transaction, consider that your wallet
address and activity will be permanently publicly visible.

================================================================================

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The Platform may link to or integrate with third-party services including:
(a) Solana RPC providers (for blockchain connectivity)
(b) Jupiter Protocol (for secondary market navigation)
(c) Vercel (hosting infrastructure)
(d) Groq (AI chat functionality, if enabled)

Each third party operates under its own privacy policy. The Company is not
responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

================================================================================

6. COOKIES

The Platform uses minimal browser storage (localStorage) for functional purposes
such as theme preferences and draft data. We do not use third-party advertising
cookies or tracking pixels.

================================================================================

7. DATA RETENTION

Usage logs are retained for up to 90 days for security and operational purposes.
Browser-stored preferences (localStorage) remain on your device until you clear
your browser data. We have no control over on-chain data, which is permanent.

================================================================================

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The Platform is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not
knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has
used the Platform, contact us at the address below.

================================================================================

outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed
in the United States. By using the Platform, you consent to this transfer.

EU residents under GDPR at this time, and accordingly the Platform is not
