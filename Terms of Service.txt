2. DESCRIPTION OF SERVICES

Mammoth Protocol is a permissionless smart contract infrastructure deployed on
the Solana blockchain. The Platform enables:

(a) Token Deployment: Users may deploy custom SPL tokens with configurable
    supply parameters, bonding curves, and treasury routing.
(b) Cycle-Based Issuance: Token creators may conduct bounded fundraising cycles
    through the Protocol's cycle management system.
(c) Rights-Based Anti-Dilution: Existing token holders may exercise pro-rata
    rights during designated windows before new cycles open to the public.
(d) Secondary Market Navigation: The Platform provides navigation to third-party
    decentralized exchanges for secondary trading of tokens.
